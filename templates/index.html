About this Syntagmatic Distractor Generation for Vocabulary Multiple Choice Questions

English vocabulary tests especially in TOEFL have 4 components such as reading passage, target word, correct answer, and distractors.

Among the fours components, distractors have the greatest impact on item difficulty (Yuni, 2018). Based on this paper there are 2 kinds of distractors based on relation with correct answer:

1. Paradigmatic

    - a word is classified under the same part of speech as the correct answer and has a semantic relationship with it
    - words A and B are paradigmatically related if they can be substituted for each other (Walter, 2001)
    - relations are between units that could (potentially) occur in the same slot (Haspelmath, 2002);
2. Syntagmatic
    - words that co-occurred with one of the words in the context
    - relations are between units that (potentially) follow each other in speech (Haspelmath, 2002);
    - words that can be combined with each other such as "cold" and "weather";

In this research, i try to generate distractors with syntagmatic type using technology namely Natural Language Processing.
